Wallis formulae


Wallis’ formula expresses π as an infinite product:

π2=∏n=1∞4⁢n24⁢n2-1=21⁢23⁢43⁢45⁢⋯

It may be derived by taking the limit as n→∞ of the ratio of the following two integrals.

∫0π2sin2⁢n⁡x⁢d⁢x=1⋅3⁢⋯⁢(2⁢n-1)2⋅4⁢⋯⁢2⁢n⁢π2
∫0π2sin2⁢n+1⁡x⁢d⁢x=2⋅4⁢⋯⁢2⁢n3⋅5⁢⋯⁢(2⁢n+1)
