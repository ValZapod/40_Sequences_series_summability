Properties of Complex Numbers

Conjugate Properties

(z1+z2)*=z1*+z2*
(z1⁢z2)*=z1*⁢z2*
(z1z2)*=z1*z2*
(zn)*=(z*)n
f⁢(z*)=f*⁢(z)
z⁢z*=|z|2

Re() and Im() Properties

z=R⁢e⁢(z)=j⁢I⁢m⁢(z)
R⁢e⁢(z)=z+z*2
I⁢m⁢(z)=z-z*2⁢j
R⁢e⁢(z*)=R⁢e⁢(z)
I⁢m⁢(z*)=-I⁢m⁢(z)
R⁢e⁢(z)≤|z|
I⁢m⁢(z)≤|z|
R⁢e⁢(z1+z2)=R⁢e⁢(z1)+R⁢e⁢(z2)
I⁢m⁢(z1+z2)=I⁢m⁢(z1)+I⁢m⁢(z2)
R⁢e⁢(z)=I⁢m⁢(j⁢z)
I⁢m⁢(z)=R⁢e⁢(-j⁢z)
