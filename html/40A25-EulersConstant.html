Euler’s constant


Euler’s constant γ is defined by

γ=limn→∞⁡(1+12+13+14+⋯+1n-ln⁡n)

or equivalently

γ=limn→∞⁡∑i=1n[1i-ln⁡(1+1i)]

Euler’s constant has the value

0.57721566490153286060651209008240243104⁢…

It is related to the gamma function by

γ=-Γ′⁢(1)

It is not known whether γ is rational or irrational.
