a series related to harmonic series


The series

∑n=1∞1n⁢nn=∑n=1∞1n1+1n (1)

is divergent.  In fact, since for every positive integer n, one has  2n>n,  i.e.  nn<2, any of the series satisfies

1n⁢nn>12⁢n.

Because the harmonic series and therefore also ∑1∞12⁢n diverges, the comparison test implies that the series (1) diverges.
