silver ratio


The silver ratio is the sum of 1 and the square root of 2, represented by the Greek letter delta with a subscript S. That is, δS=1+2, with an approximate value of 2.4142135623730950488 (see A014176 in Sloane’s OEIS). Its continued fraction is

2+12+12+12+⋱,

which suggests that the Pell numbers Pn can be used as convergents. Similarly, the nth power of the silver ratio for n>0 is Pn⁢δS+Pn-1.
