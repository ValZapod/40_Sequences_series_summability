Do the following series converge?

∑n=1∞1n2+n+1 (1)
∑n=1∞n3+n+1n4+n+1 (2)

The general of (1) may be estimated upwards:

0<1n2+n+1<1n2+0+0=1n2

Because  ∑n=1∞1n2 (an over-harmonic series) converges, then also (1) converges.

The general of (2) may be estimated downwards:

n3+n+1n4+n+1>n3+0+0n4+n4+n4=13⋅1n>0

Because ∑n=1∞13⁢1n (the harmonic series divided by 3) diverges, then also (2) diverges.
