Consider the series
\[
   \sum_{k=1}^\infty \frac 1 {k \log k}.
\]
Since the integral
\[
   \int_1^\infty \frac 1 {x \log x}\, dx 
    = \lim_{M\to \infty} \left[\log (\log(x))\right]_1^M
\]
is divergent also the series considered is divergent.
